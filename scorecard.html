Part 1 · Behavior Check

One quick read on each behavior in the Manager Activation Loop.

Identify

Are we spotting the right opportunities?

We named at least one opportunity to improve this month
We know if it's a Workflow or Capability opportunity
I made space for the team to surface ideas, not just me
Redesign

Are we improving the work, not just adding AI to it?

We ran or continued a sprint (Workflow or Capability)
We rethought the process or output, not just bolted on a tool
Something from this month is reusable (prompt, template, SOP, asset)
Reinforce

Is AI part of how we already operate?

AI came up in a team meeting or 1:1 this month
We protected time for learning or experimentation
We looked back at wins, blockers, and next steps
Enable

Do people feel safe and clear to experiment?

The team knows what's green, yellow, and red to try
I coached through at least one mistake with curiosity, not correction
Time and permission to experiment felt real, not theoretical
Recognize

Are we celebrating what's working?

I called out at least one specific AI win by name
The win was shared beyond just our team (Champs, Show & Tell, cross-functional team share-out, etc.)
Recognition pointed to a behavior we want repeated
